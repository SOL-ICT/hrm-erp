SECTION 1: TAX BRACKETS (CURRENTLY IN DATABASE)
=============================================================================

The system uses 6 progressive tax tiers:

Tier 1: NGN 0 - NGN 300,000           = 0%   (Tax-free)
Tier 2: NGN 300,000 - NGN 600,000     = 15%
Tier 3: NGN 600,000 - NGN 1,100,000   = 18%
Tier 4: NGN 1,100,000 - NGN 1,600,000 = 21%
Tier 5: NGN 1,600,000 - NGN 3,200,000 = 23%
Tier 6: NGN 3,200,000 and above       = 25%

Effective Date: January 1, 2025

NOTE: These brackets apply to the TAXABLE BALANCE after the NGN 840,000
      tax relief (see Step 4 below).

SECTION 2: TAX CALCULATION METHOD (STEP-BY-STEP)
=============================================================================

File Location: backend/app/Services/PayrollCalculationEngine.php
Method: calculateProgressiveTax() - Lines 172-254

STEP 1: Calculate Gross Income (5% Relief)
────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
Formula:    Gross Income = Annual Gross Salary × 95%
Purpose:    Standard 5% gross income relief
Example:    NGN 1,427,646.08 × 0.95 = NGN 1,356,263.78


STEP 2: Calculate Pension Relief (8%)
────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
Formula:    Pension Relief = Pensionable Amount × 8%
Purpose:    Employee pension contribution relief
Example:    NGN 1,427,646.08 × 0.08 = NGN 71,418.76


STEP 3: Calculate Taxable Income
────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
Formula:    Taxable Income = Gross Income - Pension Relief
Note:       CRA (Consolidated Relief Allowance) is NOT used
Reason:     CRA was removed from Nigerian tax law
Example:    NGN 1,356,263.78 - NGN 71,418.76 = NGN 1,284,845.02


STEP 4: Apply NGN 840,000 Tax Relief  ★ KEY STEP ★
────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
Formula:    Taxable Balance = Taxable Income - NGN 840,000
Purpose:    Flat tax-free allowance of NGN 840,000 annually
Rule:       If Taxable Income ≤ NGN 840,000 → No PAYE tax
           If Taxable Income > NGN 840,000 → Tax on balance only
Example:    NGN 1,284,845.02 - NGN 840,000 = NGN 444,845.02

           ★ ONLY NGN 444,845.02 IS SUBJECT TO TAX ★


STEP 5: Apply Progressive Tax Brackets
────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
The taxable balance (NGN 444,845.02) is now taxed progressively:

Bracket 1: NGN 0 - 300,000 @ 0%
   Taxable:  NGN 300,000.00
   Tax:      NGN 0.00

Bracket 2: NGN 300,000 - 600,000 @ 15%
   Taxable:  NGN 144,845.02  (only the amount within this bracket)
   Tax:      NGN 144,845.02 × 15% = NGN 21,726.75

────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
Total Annual PAYE Tax:  NGN 21,726.75


STEP 6: Calculate Monthly PAYE
────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
Formula:    Monthly PAYE = Annual PAYE ÷ 12
Example:    NGN 21,726.75 ÷ 12 = NGN 1,810.56

For Prorated Months:
Formula:    Prorated PAYE = Monthly PAYE × (Days Worked ÷ Total Days)
Example:    NGN 1,810.56 × (21 ÷ 30) = NGN 1,267.39
